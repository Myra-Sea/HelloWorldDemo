Breed History

  • First recognized U.S. breed of dog
  • First non-sporting breed in the U.S.
  • Originated around 1875
  • All originate from a dog named "Judge"
  • Bred down in size
  • First Boston terrier club formed in 1889
  • Became Massachusetts state dog in 1979

Physical Traits

  • Short tail
  • Small body with a compact build
  • White markings
  • "Tuxedo" is black, brindle, or dark brown
  • Square head
  • Large, round eyes
  • Short muzzle

Temperment Traits

  • People-oriented
  • Highly Intelligent
  • Alert
  • Quiet
  • Don't require much grooming
  • Moderate exercise needs
  • Easily trained for competitions
